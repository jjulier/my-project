Selfridges' Bright Young Things, October 2013

In 2013 I was invited to participate in Selfridges' Bright Young Things scheme. This involved creating and installing a window display for the department store and designing a t-shirt and range of products to be sold in the Concept Store. I titled my installation Soft Core Shopping, to highlight the pornographic quality of shopping displays - the perfection, seamless smooth shapes, and glossy surfaces, but also the grubbiness too - the settled dust and sellotaped edges. This project was sponsored by The Lurex' Company Limited. Photography by Nassima Rothacker.
